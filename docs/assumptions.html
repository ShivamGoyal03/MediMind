Assumptions

The MediMind application is based on several key assumptions:

  • Data Quality: The accuracy of the system depends heavily on the quality and completeness of the symptom-disease dataset used to train the RNN model.

  • Model Limitations: The RNN model and the LLM are not perfect and may produce incorrect predictions or recommendations.

  • User Input: The accuracy of the analysis depends on the user’s ability to accurately and completely describe their symptoms.

  • Medical Disclaimer: MediMind’s output is purely informational and should never replace professional medical advice.

  • LLM Capabilities: The LLM’s responses depend on the quality of its training data and its ability to understand medical contexts.
